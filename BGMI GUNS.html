2.UZI

        UZI is one of the most commonly used Submachine Gun in Battlegrounds Mobile India during
        the beginning of the game.

3.Groza

        Groza is a special weapon in Battlegrounds Mobile India 
        that is found only in Airdrop and nowhere on the map.

4.AWM

        AWM is a special weapon that is found only in the Airdrop crate. 
        It is the best and most powerful gun in Battlegrounds Mobile India with the maximum damage ratio.

5.AKM

AKM is one of the most powerful Assualt Rifles in Battlegrounds Mobile India. 
        It is good for close and mid-range combat.

6.M16A4

M16A4 is not much of a popular weapon but with correct handling, 
            it can cause some serious damage to the enemies

7.UMP45

            The UMP45 belongs to the SMG category and
            it is well known for its reliability and abundance across the battlefield.

8.AUG

AUG is a special Assault Rifle
             that is available only on Airdrop in Battlegrounds Mobile India.

9.SLR

  SLR (Self-Loading Rifle or Semi-Automatic Rifle) is a semi-automatic sniper rifle
             in Battleground Mobile India with 7.62 ammo.

10.SKS

  

            SKS is one of the powerful Automatic Sniper Rifles with 7.62 rounds in Battlegrounds Mobile India. Like all other Automated Sniper Rifles, 
            SKS is good at both mid-range and long-range battles.
